About Us

Nestled in the heart of Whitstable, Thirst Love Café is a bright, welcoming space where community, creativity, and great coffee come together. Whether you're stopping by for your morning flat white, meeting friends for brunch, or need a cosy corner to work from, we’ve got you covered.

We’re passionate about using locally sourced ingredients, serving up fresh bakes and seasonal specials daily. But we’re more than just a café — we’re a hub for connection, calm, and good conversation.

Come as you are, stay as long as you like, and sip something lovely while you do.
